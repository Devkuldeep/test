KYA HO TUM.....??
Tumhari surat se badhkar hai khubsurat,
Tumhari sirat se mehakti tumhari ruhaniyat!
Tumhare soch ki ye gehraiyan,
Tumhari dil ki ye masoom naadaniyan!
Kya tum ek hakikat ho,
Ya mere dil me basa ek khayal ho?
Agar tum sach ho, toh dikhaai kyu nahi dete?
Agar bharosa ho, toh aazmane kyu nhai dete?
Agar Roshni ho, toh yeh andhera kyu hai?
Agar ummeed ho, toh nirasha kyu hai?
Kya tum mere har sawal ka jawaab ho,
Ya mere har dard ki daw ho?
Aakhir bata hi do mujhe,
Tum mere kiya ho?


-SMehta
